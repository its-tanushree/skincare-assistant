🌸 Skin Routine for Dry Skin

🌞 Morning (AM) Routine

  • Gentle Hydrating Cleanser: Cleans without stripping natural oils (e.g. cream or non-foaming cleanser).
  • Hydrating Toner (Optional): Toners help add moisture and prep skin. Alcohol-free toners with ingredients like glycerin or hyaluronic acid are good for dry skin
  • Moisturizer: Rich, hydrating moisturizer with ceramides and hyaluronic acid .
  • Sunscreen (SPF 30+): Hydrating, daily sunscreen to protect and maintain moisture.

🌙 Evening (PM) Routine

  • Makeup Remover/Cleansing Oil (if needed): Removes makeup or sunscreen gently.
  • Gentle Hydrating Cleanser: Same as morning cleanser to clean skin without drying.
  • Serum (Optional) : Hydrating serum with hyaluronic acid before moisturizer for extra hydration.
  • Moisturizer: Generous application of a rich moisturizer to lock in hydration overnight.

🗓️ Weekly Routine

  • Exfoliation (1x/week): Use gentle scrub or chemical exfoliant (AHA) to remove flaky dead skin (skip if irritation occurs).
  • Hydrating Mask (1-2x/week): Moisture-boosting mask with ceramides or hyaluronic acid.
💡 Key Tips for Sensitive Skin
  • Avoid hot water while cleansing.
  • Apply moisturizer on damp skin to lock in hydration
  • Avoid harsh scrubs and alcohol-based toners.
  • Maintain consistent hydration by drinking water.

    🌸 Skin Routine for Sensitive Skin

    🌞 Morning (AM) Routine

    • Gentle, Fragrance-Free Cleanser: Hydrating, non-foaming cleanser to prevent irritation.
    • Toner: Alcohol-free, soothing toner with calming ingredients (like aloe or chamomile).
    • Serum (Optional): Hydrating serum with hyaluronic acid or niacinamide if tolerated.
    • Fragrance-Free Moisturizer: Hydrates and protects the skin barrier.
    • Sunscreen (SPF 30+): Mineral sunscreen (zinc oxide/titanium dioxide) preferred for sensitive skin.

    🌙 Evening (PM) Routine

    • Makeup Remover/Cleansing Oil (if needed): Gentle, fragrance-free makeup remover.
    • Gentle Cleanser: Same as morning cleanser.
    • Toner: Alcohol-free toner with soothing ingredients.
    • Serum (Optional): Hydrating serum if tolerated; avoid strong actives unless advised.
    • Fragrance-Free Moisturizer: Lock in hydration and restore the skin barrier.

    🗓️ Weekly Routine

    • Exfoliation (rare/1x/week): Very gentle exfoliant like lactic acid or enzymes, if tolerated.
    • Hydrating Mask (optional): Calming, fragrance-free masks to soothe irritation.
    💡 Key Tips for Sensitive Skin
    • Patch test new products before full use.
    • Avoid products with fragrance, alcohol, and harsh exfoliants.
    • Keep the routine simple and minimal.
    • Stay consistent to support long-term skin health.
